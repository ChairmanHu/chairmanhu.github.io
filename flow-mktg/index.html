Why use LifeChat?

Your residents remember a more connected family.

But the world has changed...

LifeChat bridges the gap.

Easy to get started.

A member of the family (or resident) can sign up from their mobile device.

We'll make a pairing.

They tell us a little about the resident and we'll pair them with a trained interviewer.

The family is engaged.

Before the interview, family members can send in questions.

Easy to schedule.

Once paired, the family (or resident) can easily schedule a time for the interview. We'll send reminders to the family.

Any family member can join in.

At the scheduled time, our trained interviewer will call the resident and the family can join the call. We'll ask fun questions and anyone can join in.

All their stories in one place.

And every call is preserved on a private page for the family.
